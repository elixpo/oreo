# color_splash — RGB spectrum gradient for the Color Picker

**Asset name:** `color_splash`
**Output size:** 80×49 (per PER_APP_SIZES; the app upscales 4× to 320×196)
**Used in:** `apps/color_picker/` as the 2D pickable colour field
**Theme reference:** See [THEME.md](THEME.md)

## Prompt

a clean Photoshop-style 2D colour gradient picker, the X axis sweeps a full hue rainbow from red on the far left, through orange, yellow, green, cyan, blue, magenta and back to red on the far right; the Y axis runs from PURE WHITE at the top, through fully saturated colours in the middle row, down to PURE BLACK at the bottom; smooth photographic gradient with no banding, no pixelated steps, no harsh seams between hues, no text, no letters, no UI chrome, no shapes, no logos, no faces, no watermark, fills the entire frame edge to edge, perfectly horizontal hue progression
